About Us

Little Doves Daycare Centre is a hub registered under Tanzania’s’ Child Act No.21 of 2009 and its Regulations to offer childcare services outside the family home, particularly to children who are not yet of an age to be covered by the formal school system.

We help parents on their children’s daycare services while they are at work. Our Daycare programs focus on parenting skills and child development including literacy, behavior guidance, food, nutrition, exercise, and caring for children's development, learning and health needs. Little Doves daycare Centre also offers a safest and professional daycare babysitting services.

We have comfortable, safe and fun environment which stimulates and motivates children’s social, mental and physical development.

About Us

Little Doves Daycare Centre is a hub registered under Tanzania’s’ Child Act No.21 of 2009 and its Regulations to offer childcare services outside the family home, particularly to children who are not yet of an age to be covered by the formal school system.

We help parents on their children’s daycare services while they are at work. Our Daycare programs focus on parenting skills and child development including literacy, behavior guidance, food, nutrition, exercise, and caring for children's development, learning and health needs. Little Doves daycare Centre also offers a safest and professional daycare babysitting services.

We have comfortable, safe and fun environment which stimulates and motivates children’s social, mental and physical development.

Our mission is to provide a warm and friendly environment giving exposure to all children in all fields: education, social interaction and extracurricular activities.

Our vision is to become a world class Daycare Centre that provides safe and fun environment for childcare and learning, creating a home away from home that intellectually nurtures children’s growth to their full potential

Our Core Services Objectives

Safety

To provide a safe and secure environment with quality caregivers, so that parents have the peace of mind of knowing their children are safe while they work.

Call to action

Education

To provide planned educational activities that help build a foundation for later school success.
Children learn through play as well as structured learning programs.
Most children who attend a daycare through the preschool level are able to recognize and write letters and sight words and can do basic addition and subtraction.
To provide a pre-kindergarten program; a good mix of reading, counting, art which makes the transition to kindergarten much easier, and gives children a head start on learning skills.

Call to action

Socialization

To use play and interactive activities which promotes Socialization among children.
Through play with peers and interactions with adult staff members, children learn valuable social skills including sharing, compromising and language skills.
To make sure children are well balanced socialized being. Children who do not have access to peer groups tend to have a harder time adjusting to elementary school than those that attend daycare or preschool.

Call to action

Health

To provide healthy, well-rounded meals and snacks. • To ensure children receive adequate opportunities for physical fitness through a combination of song and dance, outdoor play and structured activities.
